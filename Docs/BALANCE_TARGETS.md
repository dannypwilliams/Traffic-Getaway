# Balance Targets

## Sunset Merge Starter Compact

- Exit target: around 42s.
- Exit window: about 14s, subject to playtest.
- Average first crash: at least 30s.
- Median survival: roughly 40-55s.
- Exit appears: at least 80% of representative runs.
- Completion: 40-60% after sim/live model is trusted.
- Unfair collision estimate: under 5%.
- Near misses: about 3-8/run.
- Heavy traffic should not dominate the first 25s.
- First-time player should reasonably clear in 1-2 attempts.

## Current Measured State

Current GameSim is too easy and too generous: about 99.1% completion, 35.3 near misses/run, and 998 cash/run after core fixes.
