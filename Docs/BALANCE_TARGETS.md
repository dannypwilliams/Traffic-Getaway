# Balance Targets

Use these targets to interpret `GameSim` output. They are starting points, not permanent truth.

## Level 1: Brooklyn Warmup

- Exit should appear around 35-45 seconds; the current target is 42 seconds.
- Average first crash time should be at least 30 seconds.
- Median survival should be close to the exit timing, around 40-55 seconds.
- Exit should appear in most runs, target 80% or higher.
- Level completion should be approachable but not automatic, target 40-60% for Starter Compact.
- Unfair collision estimate should stay under 5%.
- Average near misses should be high enough to teach the reward loop, target 3-8.
- Heavy traffic should not dominate the first 25 seconds.
- Starter Bike may complete slightly more often than Starter Compact, but should earn that through lane splitting risk.

## Exit-Ramp Targets

- Level 1 should teach exit positioning, not punish late recognition.
- Early exits should leave enough time for one correction after the player notices the ramp.
- Emergency exits are beginner recovery tools and should not become the normal success route.

## Motorcycle Unlock Targets

- Starter Bike should be reachable early enough that motorcycles feel like a first-session promise.
- Motorcycle lane splitting should produce higher near-miss and combo potential, balanced by lower police resistance.
- Bike completion rates can be slightly higher than cars only when lane-split risk is being taken.

## First-Session Progression Targets

- A first-time player should reasonably clear Level 1 within 1-2 attempts.
- A decent first session should show meaningful cash progress toward the Starter Bike.
- Early rewards should encourage another run without making later vehicles feel free.

## Early New York Levels

- Level 2 should feel like a small step up, not a wall.
- Level 3 can ask for cleaner exit positioning.
- Emergency exits should help beginners recover, but should not be the normal success path.

## Vehicle Balance

- Cars should be stable and forgiving.
- Motorcycles should be more flexible but less police-resistant.
- Cash multipliers should not make one early vehicle mandatory.
- Legendary vehicles should feel better without invalidating the starter vehicles.

## Simulation Metrics To Watch

- Average survival time
- Median survival time
- First crash time
- Exit appeared rate
- Exit reached rate
- Level completion rate
- Near misses per run
- Lane splits per run
- Average cash
- Average XP
- Most common failure
- Unfair collision estimate
- Car vs motorcycle completion comparison
- Balance recommendation
