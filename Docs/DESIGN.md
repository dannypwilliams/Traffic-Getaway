# Design

## Product Target

Traffic Getaway is a one-thumb, lane-based freeway chase. The first minute must teach gap reading, movement, police pressure, and exit positioning before broader content work.

## Current Product Priorities

1. Reliable tutorial and first run.
2. Deterministic, fair traffic and exit reachability.
3. Sim/live agreement.
4. Sunset Merge balance lock.
5. Progression payoff into Starter Bike and 405 Afterburn.

## Current Guardrail

Do not add content breadth while Level 1 balance and sim/live reconciliation remain red.
